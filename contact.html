About the Author

Bio:

Meet Cheena, a 35-year-old comedy fantasy enthusiast who once tried to teach a toaster to play chess. When not busy exploring the magical realms of Random thoughts, Cheena can be found debating with real humans about the best way to make red-darter dosas. With a background in wizardry (and a minor in quantum rubber duck debugging), Cheena is on a quest to discover if when he will become a proper dad. If you have questions, riddles, or enchanted algorithms, Cheena is always ready for a new adventure!
